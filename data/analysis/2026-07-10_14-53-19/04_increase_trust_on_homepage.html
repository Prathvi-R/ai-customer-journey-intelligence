Final Consulting Report: Increasing Trust on Homepage

Executive Summary

The homepage of the Shrreeji Sharan Group website has moderate trustworthiness, with strengths in credibility, transparency, and social proof. However, critical trust signals such as testimonials, awards, and security badges are missing, which limits user confidence. Additionally, UX issues like unclear navigation, weak visual hierarchy, and accessibility gaps hinder user engagement.

To enhance trust and usability, we recommend prioritizing the addition of testimonials, certifications, and security badges, while improving navigation, CTAs, and accessibility. These changes will significantly boost credibility, user experience, and conversion rates.

Key Findings

Strengths

  1. Credibility: Mentions of "over 3 decades of experience" and community-driven projects (e.g., Covid-19 care facility).
  2. Transparency: Links to Privacy Policy, Terms & Conditions, and Copyright information.
  3. Social Proof: References to completed projects (e.g., "Shrreeji Paradise") and community events.

Weaknesses

  1. Missing Testimonials: No customer reviews or quotes to validate services.
  2. Lack of Awards & Certifications: No industry recognitions or quality certifications displayed.
  3. Limited Team Visibility: Only brief mention of Mr. Mehul Sanghavi; no detailed team profiles.
  4. No Security Signals: Absence of SSL certificates or trust seals.
  5. Weak CTAs: No prominent calls-to-action (e.g., "Schedule a Site Visit").

UX Issues

  1. Navigation: Unclear persona-based navigation and no persistent main menu.
  2. Visual Hierarchy: Competing headlines and disjointed persona-project listings.
  3. Accessibility: Low color contrast, missing alt text, and small footer font.
  4. Mobile Usability: Awkward stacking of persona sections and potential image overflow.

Opportunities

  1. Enhance Social Proof: Add testimonials, awards, and certifications to build credibility.
  2. Improve Navigation: Introduce a sticky top-nav with clear sections and dropdowns for persona-specific content.
  3. Strengthen Visual Hierarchy: Bold the hero headline, use cards/grids for personas, and highlight current projects.
  4. Boost Accessibility: Improve color contrast, add alt text, and increase footer font size.
  5. Optimize CTAs: Add prominent CTAs like "Schedule a Site Visit" and a floating "WhatsApp Chat" button.
  6. Showcase CSR: Highlight social initiatives (e.g., Covid-19 care facility) as trust badges.

Recommendations

High-Impact Recommendations

  1. Add Testimonials: Include quotes or video testimonials from satisfied customers.
  2. Display Awards & Certifications: Showcase RERA registration, ISO certifications, and industry awards.
  3. Incorporate Security Signals: Add SSL certificates or trust seals.
  4. Improve Navigation: Create a sticky top-nav with clear sections and dropdowns.
  5. Enhance CTAs: Add prominent CTAs like "Schedule a Site Visit" and "Request Callback."

Medium-Impact Recommendations

  1. Highlight CSR: Showcase social initiatives as badges (e.g., "Donated 19-Story Building for COVID Care").
  2. Improve Visual Hierarchy: Use cards/grids for personas and bold the hero headline.
  3. Boost Accessibility: Fix color contrast, add alt text, and increase footer font size.

Low-Impact Recommendations

  1. Optimize Mobile Usability: Test and fix persona stacking and image overflow issues.
  2. Format Dates: Change blog dates to natural formats (e.g., "September 22, 2025").

Priority Matrix

Priority Recommendations
High Add testimonials, display awards & certifications, incorporate security signals, improve navigation, enhance CTAs.
Medium Highlight CSR, improve visual hierarchy, boost accessibility.
Low Optimize mobile usability, format dates.

Next Steps

  1. Week 1-2: Add testimonials, awards, and certifications; incorporate security signals.
  2. Week 3-4: Redesign navigation and enhance CTAs; highlight CSR initiatives.
  3. Week 5-6: Improve visual hierarchy and accessibility; optimize mobile usability.
  4. Week 7: Test and refine all changes; monitor user engagement and conversion rates.

By implementing these recommendations, the Shrreeji Sharan Group homepage will significantly improve trust, usability, and conversion rates, positioning the brand as a leader in the real estate industry.

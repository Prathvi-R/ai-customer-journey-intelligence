# **Final Consulting Report: Ideal Customer Analysis for Shrreeji Sharan Group of Companies**  

## **Executive Summary**  
Shrreeji Sharan Group of Companies primarily serves four key customer segments: **Luxury Buyers, Families, Senior Citizens/Retirees, and Businesses**. These groups share a strong preference for **community-driven, high-quality living spaces** backed by the company’s legacy of trust. Luxury Buyers prioritize exclusivity and futuristic design, while Families seek safety and amenities. Senior Citizens value serenity and engagement, and Businesses need strategic commercial locations.  

Key opportunities include **enhancing community-centric messaging, expanding senior-friendly offerings, and addressing affordability concerns** for middle-class families.  

---

## **Key Findings**  

### **1. Luxury Buyers (High-Net-Worth Individuals)**  
- **Profile**: 35-60 years old, Mumbai-based (Western Suburbs).  
- **Motivations**: Premium architecture, exclusivity, status.  
- **Challenges**: High pricing, limited luxury inventory.  

### **2. Families (Middle to Upper-Middle Class)**  
- **Profile**: 30-50 years old, parents with children (Malad/Western Suburbs).  
- **Motivations**: Safe environment, proximity to schools/hospitals.  
- **Challenges**: Affordability, lack of large family units.  

### **3. Senior Citizens/Retirees**  
- **Profile**: 60+ years, retired professionals.  
- **Motivations**: Peaceful living, healthcare access, community events.  
- **Challenges**: Few senior-friendly housing options.  

### **4. Businesses (Commercial Buyers)**  
- **Profile**: SMEs/corporates near SV Road, Malad.  
- **Motivations**: Prime location, quality infrastructure.  
- **Challenges**: Limited flexible commercial spaces.  

### **Cross-Cutting Themes**  
✅ **Community-Centric Living** – Strong demand for multi-generational engagement.  
✅ **Trust & Legacy** – 30+ years of experience reassures buyers.  
✅ **Luxury with Purpose** – High-end design paired with soulful living.  

---

## **Opportunities**  
1. **Targeted Luxury Marketing** – Highlight exclusivity and futuristic design for HNWIs.  
2. **Family-Focused Developments** – Expand mid-range, larger family units near amenities.  
3. **Senior-Friendly Housing** – Introduce assisted-living features and community programs.  
4. **Flexible Commercial Spaces** – Offer customizable office solutions for businesses.  
5. **Affordability Solutions** – Explore flexible payment plans for middle-class buyers.

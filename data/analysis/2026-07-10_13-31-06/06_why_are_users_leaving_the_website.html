Executive Summary

The Shrreeji Sharan Group of Companies website suffers from several critical usability and trust issues that are causing users to leave prematurely. Key problems include outdated design, poor navigation, lack of essential information, and weak trust signals. Addressing these issues through targeted improvements will enhance user experience, build credibility, and increase conversions.

Key Findings

  1. Landing Experience:
  2. Strengths: Clear mission and vision, emotionally appealing messaging.

  3. Weaknesses: Outdated design, lack of visual hierarchy, no prominent hero section.

  4. Navigation:

  5. Strengths: Simple structure with basic sections.

  6. Weaknesses: Non-intuitive navigation, no categorization for different personas, missing search and filter functionality.

  7. Decision Points:

  8. Strengths: "Current Projects" section could serve as a decision point.

  9. Weaknesses: No clear pathways for users to explore properties based on their needs, missing detailed project information.

  10. Friction:

  11. Lack of detailed project information (e.g., pricing, floor plans, amenities).
  12. No clear CTAs to guide users to take the next step.
  13. Outdated blog section with unengaging content.

  14. Disclaimer undermines trust and credibility.

  15. Drop-offs:

  16. Users leave due to lack of visual appeal, missing information, poor navigation, weak CTAs, and low trust signals.

  17. Missing Information:

  18. Detailed project descriptions, pricing, and floor plans.
  19. Testimonials or case studies from previous buyers.
  20. Information about the team or leadership.

  21. Contact details or a lead capture form for inquiries.

  22. CTA Quality:

  23. CTAs are either missing or not compelling.

Opportunities

  1. Enhance Landing Experience:
  2. Add a visually appealing hero section with high-quality images and a clear tagline.

  3. Highlight key offerings prominently.

  4. Improve Navigation:

  5. Create a user-friendly menu with categories tailored to personas.

  6. Add a search bar and filters to help users find properties based on location, budget, or type.

  7. Add Decision Points:

  8. Create dedicated pages for each project with detailed information.

  9. Include a "Compare Projects" feature.

  10. Reduce Friction:

  11. Update the blog section with fresh, engaging content.

  12. Remove or rephrase the disclaimer to sound more professional and trustworthy.

  13. Prevent Drop-offs:

  14. Add testimonials, case studies, and certifications to build trust.

  15. Include a lead capture form or chatbot to engage users and capture inquiries.

  16. Enhance CTAs:

  17. Add clear, action-oriented CTAs.

  18. Use contrasting colors and persuasive language to make CTAs stand out.

  19. Provide Missing Information:

  20. Include detailed project descriptions, pricing, and timelines.
  21. Add a "Meet the Team" section to humanize the brand and build trust.

Recommendations

  1. High Priority:
  2. Redesign the homepage hero section with high-quality images and a clear tagline.
  3. Add a user-friendly menu with categories tailored to personas.
  4. Create dedicated pages for each project with detailed information.
  5. Add testimonials, case studies, and certifications to build trust.

  6. Include clear, action-oriented CTAs.

  7. Medium Priority:

  8. Update the blog section with fresh, engaging content.
  9. Remove or rephrase the disclaimer to sound more professional and trustworthy.
  10. Add a lead capture form or chatbot to engage users and capture inquiries.

  11. Include detailed project descriptions, pricing, and timelines.

  12. Low Priority:

  13. Add a "Meet the Team" section to humanize the brand and build trust.
  14. Include a "Compare Projects" feature.
